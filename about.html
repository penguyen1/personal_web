I am a first generation Vietnamese-American, born and raised in Brooklyn, New York. My role models are my parents because they are the prime examples of achieving anything through hardwork, perserverance and a smile. Growing up, I had a remarkable aptitude in math, athletics, problem-solving and interpersonal skills, which led me to take an introductory course to computer programming in college. Ever since that semester, I instantly fell in love with programming and knew that I wanted to pursue a degree and career in Computer Science.

I am an ambitious, creative, collaborative and versatile individual determined to create valuable web and end-user experiences within an organization that will lead to a lasting relationship and career in the field of Web Development. I thrive in fast-paced environments, striving to produce high quality results and continuously focus on finding the best methods to build exceptional products.
